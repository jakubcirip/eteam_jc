Improve your image in front of your customers and candidates as well. Checkli brings following benefits:

Benefits for your company

Scalability

On our platform, you can process more job positions at the same time. Also, multiple HR specialists can work on the same interview at the same time.

"Just" a tool

Let a technology analyse the content and you decide based on your human feelings. We are not trying to replace your human resources sector, we are trying to help them!

Reduce the fluctuation

Our platform can provide enough information for you to make sure the candidate understands a language on the level you require him. Also, you can test some of our example scenarios to see if the candidate can respond as you expect him to.

Benefits for the candidate

Submit from anywhere

Candidate can handle the interview from anywhere and anywhen they want. No matter if you submit the interview from home or on the train, no matter if you decide to use smartphone or laptop, eveerything will be working as expected.

Follow the technology

Today's candidates are familiar with modern technologies and they will appreciate compaines that take advanged of the technology.

Video CV

You can offer the candidates a video interview for submiting the interview results by exporting it from our platform. This may help you get interest from more candidates as they get a reward even if they won't get hired.
